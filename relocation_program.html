Relocation Smart Program

Smart Booking Engine Algorithm That Combines Split Availability to Create Full Hotel Stays and Increase Revenue

Concept

Hotels often lose bookings when a room is unavailable for the full stay. However, availability may exist across different room types or sister hotels. The Relocation Smart Program intelligently combines these availability gaps to offer guests a complete stay while clearly informing them of any room or hotel change.
