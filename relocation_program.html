Relocation Smart Program

Smart Booking Engine Algorithm That Combines Split Availability to Create Full Hotel Stays and Increase Revenue

Concept

Hotels often lose bookings when a room is unavailable for the full stay. However, availability may exist across different room types or sister hotels. The Relocation Smart Program intelligently combines these availability gaps to offer guests a complete stay while clearly informing them of any room or hotel change.

The Problem

Example scenario: Guest searches: Check-in: **1 Feb** Check-out: **10 Feb** Twin Room Availability 1–4 Feb 🟢 Available 4–10 Feb 🔴 Sold Out Twin Room Availability Traditional booking engines show: **“No Availability”** Even though **the hotel actually has 10 nights available** across room types.

The Solution

The Smart Algorithm detects the split availability and offers: Stay Option 1–4 Feb Twin Room 4–10 Feb Double Room Stay Option 1–4 Feb Twin Room 4–10 Feb Double Room Hotel A 1–4 Feb Available Hotel B (same group) 4–10 Feb Available Hotel A 1–4 Feb Available The system generates **one combined reservation confirmation**.

Benefits

Hotels
  • Fill gap nights
  • Increase occupancy
  • Reduce lost bookings
Booking Engines
  • More successful searches
  • Higher conversion
  • More commission revenue
Guests
  • Stay in preferred hotel
  • Transparent booking options
  • More availability choices
